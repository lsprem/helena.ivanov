The 4-Phase Propaganda Model

Phase I: Narrative Shaping

The creation of "Us vs. Them" binaries through strategic media framing.

Phase II: Historical Weaponization

Manipulating trauma to justify revenge as a moral necessity.

Phase III: Dehumanization

Stripping targets of human agency via objectifying media discourse.

Phase IV: Mobilization

Translating words into state-led violence against civilian populations.
