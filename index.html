The Ivanov Propaganda Model

Developed at LSE: Inside Propaganda: Serbian Media in the Yugoslav Wars

01. Key Phases

The model identifies the progressive steps of media manipulation, from initial narrative shaping to the active justification of civilian targeting.

02. Functions

Analyzing how propaganda serves to mobilize supporters, silence dissent, and create a moral vacuum for state-led violence.

03. Discourses

Focuses on "Us vs. Them" narratives, the weaponization of history, and the strategic construction of victimhood.

04. Techniques

Deconstructs the specific rhetorical tools used to dehumanize targets and normalize exclusion.

"Commended by examiners for making a novel and distinct contribution to the study of propaganda and violence... applicable to modern conflicts in Ukraine and the Middle East."
