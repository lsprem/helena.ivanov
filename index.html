The 4-Phase Propaganda Model

Phase I: Narrative Shaping

The creation of "Us vs. Them" binaries. Identifying target populations as existential threats to core collective identity.

Phase II: Historical Weaponization

Revising history to justify revenge. Narratives of perpetual victimhood are used to morally sanction pre-emptive violence.

Phase III: Dehumanization

The removal of human agency. Language transitions from political disagreement to describing targets as objects or vermin.

Phase IV: Mobilization

The transition from word to action. Organized state or group violence is normalized and executed as a "defensive" necessity.
