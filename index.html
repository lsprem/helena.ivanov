About Dr. Ivanov

Dr. Helena Ivanov is an Associate Research Fellow at the Henry Jackson Society. She recently completed her PhD at the London School of Economics, following an MPhil in Political Theory at the University of Oxford and a BA in Politics at the University of Belgrade.

Her work bridges the gap between high-level academic theory and the front lines of international media. She is a frequent commentator on global security, appearing on major outlets to provide clarity on the most pressing geopolitical crises of our time.
